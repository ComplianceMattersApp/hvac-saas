# GTM Owner-Led Success Guide V1

Status: ACTIVE onboarding support guide
Purpose: help new and active customers run real work quickly without feeling overwhelmed.

## 1) What this guide is for

Use this guide when someone says, "I have the app, now what?"

This guide is for:
- New trial customers
- Active customers who still want setup or training help

The goal is simple: run one real job end to end, then repeat the daily routine.

## 2) The simple success path

Follow this path first:
- Customer
- Job
- Schedule
- Field notes
- Closeout
- Invoice

If this path is working, the account is on track.

## 3) First 30-minute walkthrough

Use this script with the owner and core team:

1. Start here
- "Today we are not setting up everything. We are running one real job."

2. Confirm company details
- Open Admin Center and Company Profile.
- Confirm company name and support contact info.

3. Invite your team
- Invite office/dispatch and at least one tech.

4. Create first customer
- Add one real customer your team is working with now.

5. Create first job
- Add one real job tied to that customer.

6. Schedule and assign
- Put the job on the schedule and assign the tech.

7. Field notes
- Have the tech open the job page and add notes while work is happening.

8. Closeout and invoice
- Move the work to closeout and handle the invoice step.

9. Set the daily habit
- "Each morning, open Today/Ops and clear what needs attention first."

## 4) First week checklist

Use this checklist during week one:
- Company details confirmed
- Team invited
- First customer created
- First job created
- Job scheduled and assigned
- Field notes captured
- Work moved to closeout
- Invoice handled
- Today/Ops used each morning

## 5) Daily routine

Morning:
- Open Today/Ops
- Confirm what must be handled first
- Check schedule and assignment gaps

Midday:
- Clean up schedule and assignment
- Confirm field updates are being captured on jobs

End of day:
- Review closeout queue
- Complete invoice follow-up
- Leave tomorrow with clear priorities

## 6) Role quick starts

Owner/Admin:
- Do first: confirm setup basics and team access
- Focus pages: Admin Center, Today, Ops

Dispatcher/Office:
- Do first: keep schedule and assignment clean
- Focus pages: Today, Ops queues

Technician:
- Do first: open assigned jobs and add field notes
- Focus pages: Today, Job page

Billing/AR:
- Do first: keep closeout to invoice handoff moving
- Focus pages: Invoice pages and payment views

ECC/Rater:
- Do first: keep testing and exception follow-up moving
- Focus pages: Today, Ops exceptions, Job page

## 7) What can wait

Do not try to finish everything at once. These can wait:
- Advanced reports
- Service plans unless needed now
- Payment automation
- Contractor collaboration
- Deep settings

## 8) End-of-trial / success check-in questions

Use these at the end of trial, or at the end of a training week:
- Can your team run customer to invoice without confusion?
- Are jobs being scheduled and assigned daily?
- Are tech notes captured on the job page?
- Is closeout and invoice follow-up happening each day?
- Does the team start each morning in Today/Ops?
- What is still slowing the team down?

## 9) Support and feedback note

If something feels confusing, tell Eddie what you were trying to do and where you got stuck.
